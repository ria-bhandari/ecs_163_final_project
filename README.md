# ECS 163 Final Project - Is Climate Change Occurring Uniformly Across the World

Visualizing the impacts of climate change through temperature and rising sea levels.

## Goal of this Project
To show the effects of climate change is non-uniform by diving deeper into sea level rise and increase in average temperature. This will allow us to compare across different regions and years to better understand the impact of climate change. 

## Main Visualizations
1. Interactive geographical map: shows surface temperature changes over years and regions
2. Interactive line charts that shows the pattern between sea surface height changes and surface temperature changes 
3. Stream graph that shows regions of high surface temparature and/or high sea levels

The focus is on visualization 1 above. There is a time slider that can help to compare over years and a zoom in to California and other regions. There is some focus on California since there is a lot of data for that.
